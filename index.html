Simon Says is a captivating memory game that puts players' recollection skills to the test. The game begins with Simon, the virtual opponent, displaying a sequence of colors, such as red, green, blue, and yellow, in a specific order. Players must then replicate the sequence by clicking on corresponding color buttons. With each successful reproduction, the game becomes progressively more challenging, introducing longer and more complex color sequences. Players rely on their memory to accurately recall the order of the colors and advance to higher levels.
